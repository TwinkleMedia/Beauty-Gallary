About Us

Founders of Beauty Salon & Academy, Gayatri Joshi and Neha Kaur, are seasoned beauty experts and certified entrepreneurs based in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Together, they bring over 13 years of combined experience in makeup, skincare, and hair education, empowering individuals to excel in the beauty industry through top-notch training and mentorship.


Expertise and Global Exposure:Neha Kaur & Gayatri Joshi with over a decade of expertise, has participated in prestigious national and international master classes, including Makeup Master Classes in Pune (2009) and UK (2017- 2022), a Russian Hairstyle Class in Delhi, Brazilian Makeup Artist Master Classes in Delhi and Mumbai (2021), and the International Pro Master Class in Delhi.

Neha Kaur, with 10 years of extensive experience, is recognized for her creative artistry and innovative beauty techniques. Her dedication to refining skills and staying ahead of trends has made her a sought-after professional in the beauty and education industry. Together, Gayatri and Neha continue to set high standards, inspiring and nurturing the next generation of beauty professionals.
